Ingredients

  • 5 lbs oxtails
  • 2lbs all-purpose flour

  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 2 celery stalks diced
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp allspice

  • 3 cups Jerk BBQ Sauce
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 3 fresh thyme sprigs
